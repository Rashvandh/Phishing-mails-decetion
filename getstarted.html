Welcome to the Phishing Email Detection Tool

What is Phishing?

Phishing is a type of cyberattack where a malicious actor attempts to trick individuals into revealing sensitive information, such as passwords, credit card numbers, or login credentials. These attacks often occur through deceptive emails that appear to come from trusted sources.

How This Tool Helps

This tool helps detect phishing attempts by analyzing email content for suspicious characteristics like harmful links, forged sender information, and more. Simply upload an email file and let the tool help you identify potential phishing threats!

How to Get Started

  1. Click on the "Get Started" button below.
  2. You will be directed to the email upload page.
  3. Upload an email file (e.g., .eml) for analysis.
  4. Review the results to see if the email is suspicious or safe.
